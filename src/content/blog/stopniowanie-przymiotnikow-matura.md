Stopniowanie przymiotników (comparatives and superlatives) to temat, który na maturze pojawia się w zadaniach z transformacją zdań. Zasady są proste — wystarczy zapamiętać kiedy dodajemy -er/-est, a kiedy używamy more/most.

## Stopień wyższy (comparative)

### Krótkie przymiotniki (1-2 sylaby) → -er + than

| Przymiotnik | Stopień wyższy |
|---|---|
| tall | taller than |
| fast | faster than |
| big | bigger than (podwójna spółgłoska) |
| nice | nicer than (kończy się na -e) |
| happy | happier than (-y → -ier) |

### Długie przymiotniki (3+ sylaby) → more + than

| Przymiotnik | Stopień wyższy |
|---|---|
| beautiful | more beautiful than |
| interesting | more interesting than |
| expensive | more expensive than |
| comfortable | more comfortable than |

## Stopień najwyższy (superlative)

### Krótkie → the + -est

*tall → the tallest, big → the biggest, happy → the happiest*

### Długie → the most

*beautiful → the most beautiful, interesting → the most interesting*

## Formy nieregularne

| Podstawowy | Wyższy | Najwyższy |
|---|---|---|
| good | better | the best |
| bad | worse | the worst |
| much/many | more | the most |
| little | less | the least |
| far | further/farther | the furthest/farthest |
| old | older / elder | the oldest / the eldest |

## Przydatne konstrukcje

**As...as** (tak samo jak):
*She is as tall as her brother.*
*This film is not as good as the book.*

**The + comparative, the + comparative:**
*The harder you work, the better results you get.*

**Comparative + and + comparative** (coraz bardziej):
*It's getting colder and colder.*
*The prices are becoming more and more expensive.*

## Najczęstsze błędy maturzystów

**Błąd 1: Podwójny stopień wyższy**
❌ *She is more taller than me.*
✅ *She is taller than me.*

**Błąd 2: Brak "the" przy superlatywie**
❌ *She is best student in class.*
✅ *She is the best student in class.*

**Błąd 3: Nieregularne formy**
❌ *This film is more good than the other one.*
✅ *This film is better than the other one.*

**Błąd 4: "than" zamiast "as"**
❌ *She is as tall than her sister.*
✅ *She is as tall as her sister.*

## 10 zadań w stylu maturalnym (z kluczem)

**Zadanie 1.** My brother is \_\_\_\_ (tall) than me.
**Zadanie 2.** This is \_\_\_\_ (expensive) restaurant in the city.
**Zadanie 3.** Today is \_\_\_\_ (bad) day of my life.
**Zadanie 4.** She runs \_\_\_\_ (fast) than anyone in her class.
**Zadanie 5.** This book is not as \_\_\_\_ (interesting) as the film.
**Zadanie 6.** The weather is getting \_\_\_\_ and \_\_\_\_ (cold).
**Zadanie 7.** \_\_\_\_ you practise, \_\_\_\_ you improve. (more/better)
**Zadanie 8.** He is \_\_\_\_ (good) player on the team.
**Zadanie 9.** This exercise is \_\_\_\_ (difficult) than the previous one.
**Zadanie 10.** She has \_\_\_\_ (little) patience than her sister.

## Klucz odpowiedzi
