Spójniki i wyrażenia łączące (linking words / connectors) to element, który pojawia się na maturze zarówno w zadaniach gramatycznych jak i w wypowiedzi pisemnej. Dobra znajomość spójników podnosi ocenę za esej i list.

## Spójniki przyczynowe (cause and effect)

| Spójnik | Użycie | Przykład |
|---|---|---|
| because | zdanie podrzędne | *I stayed home because it was raining.* |
| because of | + rzeczownik | *I stayed home because of the rain.* |
| since / as | zdanie podrzędne | *Since it was late, we left.* |
| therefore | nowe zdanie | *It was raining. Therefore, we stayed home.* |
| as a result | nowe zdanie | *It rained. As a result, the match was cancelled.* |
| due to | + rzeczownik | *The delay was due to bad weather.* |
| consequently | nowe zdanie | *He didn't study. Consequently, he failed.* |

## Spójniki kontrastowe (contrast)

| Spójnik | Użycie | Przykład |
|---|---|---|
| but | łączy zdania | *I wanted to go, but I was tired.* |
| however | nowe zdanie | *I wanted to go. However, I was tired.* |
| although / though | zdanie podrzędne | *Although I was tired, I went.* |
| even though | zdanie podrzędne (nacisk) | *Even though it rained, we had fun.* |
| despite | + rzeczownik/-ing | *Despite the rain, we had fun.* |
| in spite of | + rzeczownik/-ing | *In spite of being tired, she worked.* |
| nevertheless | nowe zdanie | *It was expensive. Nevertheless, I bought it.* |
| on the other hand | nowe zdanie | *It's expensive. On the other hand, it's good quality.* |

## Spójniki celu (purpose)

| Spójnik | Przykład |
|---|---|
| to / in order to | *I study hard to pass the exam.* |
| so that | *I study hard so that I can pass.* |
| so as to | *She left early so as to avoid traffic.* |

## Spójniki czasu (time)

| Spójnik | Przykład |
|---|---|
| when | *Call me when you arrive.* |
| while | *I read while she was cooking.* |
| after | *After finishing dinner, we went for a walk.* |
| before | *Brush your teeth before going to bed.* |
| until | *Wait until I get back.* |
| as soon as | *Call me as soon as you can.* |

## Spójniki dodające (addition)

| Spójnik | Przykład |
|---|---|
| and | *She is smart and hardworking.* |
| also / as well | *She is also very kind.* |
| moreover / furthermore | *It's cheap. Moreover, it's good quality.* |
| in addition | *In addition to being smart, she's kind.* |
| not only... but also | *She is not only smart but also kind.* |

## Najczęstsze błędy maturzystów

**Błąd 1: "despite" + zdanie**
❌ *Despite it was raining, we went out.*
✅ *Despite the rain, we went out.* / *Although it was raining, we went out.*

**Błąd 2: "because of" + zdanie**
❌ *I was late because of I missed the bus.*
✅ *I was late because I missed the bus.* / *I was late because of the bus.*

**Błąd 3: Przecinek po "however"**
❌ *However she was tired she continued.*
✅ *However, she was tired, she continued.*

## 10 zadań w stylu maturalnym (z kluczem)

**Zadanie 1.** \_\_\_\_ the bad weather, the match took place. (mimo)
**Zadanie 2.** She studied hard. \_\_\_\_, she failed the exam. (jednak)
**Zadanie 3.** I left early \_\_\_\_ avoid the traffic. (żeby)
**Zadanie 4.** \_\_\_\_ being expensive, the hotel was excellent. (mimo)
**Zadanie 5.** The project was cancelled \_\_\_\_ lack of funding. (z powodu)
**Zadanie 6.** She not only speaks French \_\_\_\_ German too.
**Zadanie 7.** \_\_\_\_ you arrive, please call me. (jak tylko)
**Zadanie 8.** The film was boring. \_\_\_\_, we stayed until the end.
**Zadanie 9.** I need to study more \_\_\_\_ I can pass the exam. (żeby)
**Zadanie 10.** \_\_\_\_ she was tired, she kept working. (chociaż)
